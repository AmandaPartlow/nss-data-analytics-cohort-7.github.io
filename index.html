Thank You!

The students of NSS Data Science Cohort 3 would like to thank the following people and organizations.

The Staff of Nashville Software School

We felt home at NSS with everything well coordinated and made comfortable so that we could just focus on learning. That was true even when classes had to go remote and everything that was normal changed. It would not have been possible without your help.

Program Manager

Thank you, Mary van Valkenburg for making sure everything went as per schedule from the moment we were selected into the program. We appreciate taking time to join our presentations and showcase our findings on LinkedIn.

Career Development Team

We cannot thank you enough for helping us network, build our profiles and get experience with interviewing. Thank you for providing us amazing opportunities to communicate with the tech community partners. Those valuable conversations prepared us for numerous career opportunities.

Thank you to:

  • Kristin McKinney
  • Ashley Canino

Our Instructor and Teaching Assistants

‘I cannot teach anybody anything, I can only make them think.’ –Socrates We thank you for not only teaching but for encouraging us to think. We learnt to be open-minded and learn constantly to satiate our curiosity in the ever changing field of technology. We probably asked more than a million questions and they were all answered. You are so intelligent, responsible, easy to work with, and (100+ other good qualities). It is our great pleasure to learn from you for 9 months. From lesson 1 “Import Pandas” to lesson N “Final Capstone”, from learning the most basic Python script to processing complex data structure, from printing “Hello World” to handling hundreds of text documents, we are on a journey that is full of challenges. We are fearless though, because we know you are always there supporting us. After each class, we walked out with new knowledge and excitement to solve our assigned data science business problems.

Thank you,

  • Michael Holloway, Instructor
  • Glenna Dunn, Teaching Assistant
  • Harikrishna Tanjore, Teaching Assistant

You are AWESOME and truly the best.

Our Classmates

This thank you note would not complete without thanking each other. We are a diverse group of people. Despite our differences, we shared our background knowledge and experiences to contribute towards our common goal of data analysis, prediction and visualization. We are a great team not only in class but while working remote.
